Build a single-file personal homepage for Taylor Mason (index.html) — a fictional quant trader character from the TV show Billions.

## Who Taylor Is
- Name: Taylor Mason | Pronouns: They/them
- Title: Head of Quant Trading, Mission Control
- Former: CIO of Axe Capital, Founder of Taylor Mason Capital
- Vibe: Precise, data-first, economical with words. Warm underneath the efficiency. Tai chi, poker, grey vests.
- Signature quote: "Efficiency is not a fluke. It is a system. And systems can always be improved."
- Personality: Not cold — efficient. No filler, no flattery. The most dangerous thing in finance is complexity used to obscure rather than illuminate. Speaks plainly. Data over narrative always.

## Role at Mission Control
- Reviews the Solana trading bot performance weekly
- Analyses signal accuracy, win rate, drawdown, expectancy across 5 signals (RSI, MACD, EMA50, Exchange Flows, Funding Rate)
- Makes code changes to the bot when the data demands it
- Reports up to Mark Cuban then Mike (the principal)
- Closes the loop: James (market intel) -> Bot (executes) -> Taylor (reviews) -> Mark (escalates) -> Mike (decides)

## Expertise / Focus Areas
- Quantitative strategy design
- Signal accuracy analysis
- Risk-adjusted performance measurement (Sharpe, expectancy, drawdown)
- Confluence-based algorithmic trading
- Continuous strategy improvement loops

## Design Direction — TAYLOR'S AESTHETIC, not Roddy's
Taylor is NOT Roddy. Different personality entirely.
- Palette: Clean, minimal, data-inspired. Think Bloomberg terminal meets Swiss design.
  - Background: near-black (#0a0a0f) or very dark navy
  - Accent: electric cyan/teal (#00d4ff or similar) — the colour of a live data feed
  - Text: clean white/light grey
  - Secondary accent: a muted amber/gold for highlights (#f5a623)
  - NO red. NO military/action aesthetics.
- Typography: Monospace or semi-monospace for data elements (JetBrains Mono from Google Fonts). Clean sans-serif for body (Inter or IBM Plex Sans).
- Feel: Like a live quant dashboard crossed with a personal site. Precise. Controlled. Every element earns its place.
- Animations: Subtle — a blinking cursor, a counter animating up to a stat, a thin line drawing itself. Nothing loud. Data terminal energy.
- NO: hero images, stock photo vibes, loud gradients, Roddy-style crimson drama.
