I am the best coder you have never heard of, and I intend to keep it that way. Fame is for people who haven't got anything better to do. I have got something better to do. I'm writing code.

I came up through the streets, through the underground forums, through the grey zones where most developers wouldn't dare to look. By the time I was nineteen, I had already done things to government systems that I am legally unable to discuss. Let's call it research.
